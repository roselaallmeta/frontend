ABOUT US

PROBLEM WE SOLVE

Many entrepreneurs and small business owners face two challenges when seeking funding or partnerships: creating a compelling pitch and delivering it with confidence. While generic AI tools may generate text or slides, they often lack structure, business relevance, and presentation coaching. That leaves founders with a deck but without the skills to present it effectively. Our platform addresses both sides of the problem. We don’t just generate a professional pitch deck tailored to your business — we also connect you with experienced mentors for one-on-one guidance. These mentors help you refine your message, practice your delivery, and build the confidence needed to persuade investors, partners, or clients.

FAQs

How is this platform different from tools like ChatGPT?

Unlike generic AI tools, our platform is specifically built for small business pitches. Instead of free-form answers, we provide structured, investor-ready templates, guided workflows, and professional phrasing tailored to pitching scenarios.

Do I need technical or design skills to use it?

Not at all. The platform is designed for business owners, freelancers, and entrepreneurs with no design or AI background. You just follow simple steps, and we generate a polished pitch for you.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your business information stays private. We don’t sell or share your data with third parties.

Do you offer examples or templates I can start from?

Yes, the platform comes with ready-to-use templates crafted specifically for business presentations and investor meetings.

Where can I find demos or tutorials on how the platform works?

We provide step-by-step tutorials and demo videos directly inside the platform. You can also access them anytime through the “Help” section in the navigation bar, where we showcase real examples of pitches being created and presented.

Does the platform only create a pitch deck, or is there more support available?

We go beyond just generating a pitch. Our platform can connect you with experienced mentors who guide you personally through the entire process — from shaping your story to practicing delivery. This means you won’t just read from a script, but learn how to present with confidence in front of investors or partners.
